# Brand Brief: Baby Facts Unlocked

## Value Proposition
Mind-blowing baby facts and infant development insights delivered as bite-sized, entertaining YouTube Shorts. The channel unlocks the fascinating science behind babies for parents, caregivers, and curious minds.

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: New and expecting parents (25-40 years old)
- **Secondary**: Caregivers, grandparents, early childhood educators
- **Tertiary**: Curious minds who love surprising science facts
- **Pain points**: Information overload, anxiety about baby milestones, wanting to understand their baby better
- **What they want**: Quick, trustworthy, entertaining baby knowledge they can share

## Tone of Voice
- Fun and curious — like a friend who just learned something amazing
- Mind-blowing — lean into the "wait, really?!" reaction
- Warm and accessible — never clinical or condescending
- Confident but not preachy — share facts, not judgments
- Slightly playful — babies are inherently delightful

## Visual Identity
- Bright, warm colors (soft yellows, pastels, warm whites)
- Clean, bold text overlays for key facts
- Friendly, rounded typography
- Baby-themed visual elements without being overly cutesy

## Key Messages
1. Babies are more amazing than you think
2. Science reveals surprising things about newborns
3. Understanding your baby helps you parent better
4. Baby development is fascinating, not scary
5. Every baby fact is worth sharing

## Content Pillars
| Pillar | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Surprising Facts | 30% | "Did you know..." style mind-blowers |
| Development Milestones | 20% | What babies can do at different ages |
| Parenting Hacks | 20% | Science-backed tips for new parents |
| Baby Science | 20% | How baby bodies and brains work |
| Myth-Busting | 10% | Common baby myths debunked |

## Hashtags
Primary: #BabyFacts #Shorts
Secondary: #NewbornTrivia #InfantDevelopment #ParentingTips #BabyScience #NewParents #BabyMilestones

## Platform
YouTube Shorts only (1080x1920, 9:16, 15-45 seconds, AI voiceover)
